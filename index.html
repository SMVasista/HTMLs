Welcome to RR Turbo - The Interfaced Accelerator for RR tools

RR is a Cellworks® centered, auxillary development platform comprising of 45+ (and counting) modeling and quality check specific tools to make the overall process automatic, hassel-free and stream-lined for development pipelines. You can access all the below features and generate/perform specific functions as needed. To know more about RR click here
